The Y4Y Youth Pledge

Every organization that joins the directory commits to our shared values of inclusion, collaboration, and youth empowerment.

  • ✊ Respect the dignity of every young person
  • 🌱 Commit to sustainable and inclusive programming
  • 🤝 Engage in authentic cross-cultural collaboration
  • 📣 Amplify marginalized youth voices
  • 🔍 Maintain transparency in operations
📋 View Extended Pledge Bylaws & Policy Framework

By committing to this digital charter, structural youth organizations formally align with the Y4Y Model of Youth Governance. Signatories actively transition from traditional volunteer models to establish recognized stakeholder seats in regional structural development, project management, and global policy design.

🔍 Weekly Compliance & Review Mandate

Submission of your directory profile initiates an ongoing quality-assurance framework. The Y4Y administrative board runs meticulous weekly audits of all active directory entries. To ensure directory health, entries containing inappropriate content, insufficient information, or an established false presence will face disciplinary steps.

Following due notification and an observation window, non-compliant profiles will be permanently deleted from the active directory database to protect our global network's alignment and collaborative integrity.
